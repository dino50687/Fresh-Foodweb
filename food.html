Spaghetti Carbonara

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. In a skillet, cook the bacon until crispy. Add the butter, garlic, and cream. Simmer for 2 minutes. Add the Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper. Toss the spaghetti with the sauce and serve.
